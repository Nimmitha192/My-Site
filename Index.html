ABOUT US

Golden Crust Pizzeria is located in Hapugala, Galle, Sri Lanka, and is dedicated to serving fresh, delicious, and high-quality pizzas. We use carefully selected ingredients, freshly prepared dough, and rich sauces to create great-tasting pizzas. Whether you dine in or take away, we focus on excellent flavor, friendly service, and customer satisfaction. We are proud to serve our local community with food made with passion and care.
